✨Wellness Journey✨

During my time at i.c.stars, I had moments where self-doubt crept in—especially when I was faced with learning something completely new. I caught myself questioning whether I could keep up with my peers. Instead of letting that fear take over, I chose to lean into the challenge, ask questions, and remind myself that growth often begins in discomfort. Looking back now, I see those moments not as setbacks, but as stepping stones that made me stronger and more resilient.
I often struggled with imposter syndrome during my time at i.c.stars, telling myself I wasn’t skilled enough to be there. Over time, I learned to challenge those thoughts by reframing them. Instead of saying, “I don’t belong here,” I reminded myself, “I am learning, growing, and contributing in my own way.” Each day, I proved to myself that I did belong, and my progress became evidence of that truth.
